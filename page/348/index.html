Modern Art

Posted on March 28, 2005 by Foton
Reply

Never underestimate the value of subtle wit.

Over the weekend, I was passing through Ironforge (slowly, ever so lagfully slowly) when I ran into a naked, green orc corpse floating in midair by the Auction House bridge. Haha, thought I — I never tire of visual humor.

Then I looked closer. It was another player’s visual tribute to Scamsalot and the inexplicable delays in banning his rotten ass from our server.

Another hour or two later, I was again inching my way through Ironforge and the same player had made another visual tribute to Scamsalot, this time on top of the bank mailbox.

Well played, mystery Ironforge artist. Well played.
