Games for Reviews

Posted on March 13, 2006 by Foton
Reply

Free Video Games for Bloggers: review one of Binary Moon’s downloadable games, post a review (either positive or negative) on ye olde blog, receive a full version of the game for free. I was going to pine, in verse, for this business model to spread to all games in the future, but it occurs to me that this IS the business model for the adver-zines.

OMG I Hate U 2

Posted on March 10, 2006 by Foton
1

From the Warcraft forums, a dwarf hunter complains about the price of celebrity connected to his much coveted, crazy-rare, CURSED Baron Rivendare’s Mount. (Warcraft noobs: Here’s the Baron’s Deathcharger mount) He explains: “I don’t mind the whispers, but being told about 20-30 times a day that I am hated is not so fun. Of course they mean it in jest, as in, ‘OMG is that the barons mount? I hate u! U lucky SOB, grats’. This hurts me and I sometimes cry myself to sleep.” (Furl archive to use when live thread is dead. Update: Never mind Furl, it’s dead too.)
