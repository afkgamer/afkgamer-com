The World Waits for the FedEx Guy (or Gal!)

Posted on January 15, 2007 by Foton

This is fun: watching the growing panic on the World of Warcraft forums as the world (of warcraft) anxiously awaits delivery of The Burning Crusade.

Here’s some to get you started:

EBGames.com – “No more processing” (Probably 2 million people pumping the My Orders button on the EBGames site has destroyed their servers.)
Best Buy preorder issues – Blizzard speak to BB
Who here is outside a gamestop w/ tent? (wtf)
Amazon Shipping Info
Gamestop Warehouses Delayed: Ice Storm (Can’t Blizzard call God and have Him melt the ice? Crimony, doesn’t anyone work over there?!?!?!)

UPDATE: On the EBgames.com website >> My Orders:

Due to system maintenance our order status system is temporarily unavailable. Please do not call our customer service center as they share the same system and do not have additional information. We apologize for the inconvenience; please check back later.

World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade orders are shipping today, on schedule, out of our Louisville, Kentucky distribution center. If you placed an order before 2:00pm (cst) on Monday January 15, 2007, and have NOT received an email from us requesting additional payment processing information, your Burning Crusade order should ship today at the shipping service level you requested.

We Warcrafters are like a swarm of locusts, we destroy everything in our path.
