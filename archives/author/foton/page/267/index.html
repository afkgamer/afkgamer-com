Road Trip!!

Posted on September 14, 2005 by Foton
Reply

At long last, we have the tools at our disposal to bring our online disputes directly to the people responsible. Oh sure, it was POSSIBLE before if one was willing to do copious amounts of research and google all over the damn place, but now! it’s as easy as clicking around on a map.

Bad coding, inadequate hardware, nonexistent customer service, corrupt databases, evil development choices — these have driven us all to threaten: I OUGHT TO DRIVE DOWN THERE MYSELF AND BEAT THEM WITH A STAPLER!

The ultimate Game Developer Stalker Map, complete with driving directions. I noticed that users can add comments, too, which is really just asking for trouble. (Yes, I know it’s tempting, but don’t do it.)

Not that I advocate stapler violence. That would be wrong. Spitballs or a nerf ball in the back of the head wouldn’t be so bad though.

Best. Stalker. Webtool. Ever.

(Found over at J’s place, damned vulpine; and he found the best stalker webtool ever at Massively Multi-Player Game Development.)
