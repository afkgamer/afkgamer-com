The Bizzaro Blizzard World

Posted on May 26, 2005 by Foton
2

I swear, it must be like talking to a brick wall over there at Castle Blizzard. One of the suits walks to the khaki area, he (she) makes a few remarks, chastizes the worker bees, reminds them of this, that or the other priority and looks out at a sea of blank faces.

Anything? Is anything getting through?

A recent post by Abalieno of The Cesspit discusses the new! and improved! battlegrounds patch, and includes a link to some battlegrounds info courtesy of Furor. On the FoHror boards. (UPDATE 6/05: FoHror link is dead. Why, it’s almost as if it never happened!)

Doesn’t this seem oh so familiar? Yes, yes it does. Before you refresh your memory, I should warn you that the link to Blizzard’s response at the end is no longer operational. I’m sure that’s just a coincidence. Maybe it was removed/moved to save fa .. space or something.

Nevertheless, I’m just really curious what kind of an operation they have over there. Shit. When my boss says do this, don’t do that, can’t you read the signs?, he doesn’t have to tell me twice. Color me obedient, I like to get paid.

Any parents looking to get a job at Blizzard, they have nice benefits. Sounds like it’s one big daycare center.
