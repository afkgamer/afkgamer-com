The AQ War Effort

Posted on January 6, 2006 by Foton

Warcraft patch 1.9 introduced that colossal server-wide quest to open the gates of Ahn’Qiraj — it primarily involves pulling 3.85 million resources out of the economy. (I’ll be keeping my runecloth and thorium, thankyouverymuch.) You can watch the server races at Ahn’Qiraj War Supplies Server Rankings. Since that site doesn’t include details of how the census data is polled, I can’t comment on the accuracy other than the totals required is accurate and my own server’s status is approximately correct. Details of the resource requirements can be found at WoW Insider.
