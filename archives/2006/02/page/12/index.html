Gamers are Smerter

Posted on February 14, 2006 by Foton

Gamers consistently outperform non-gamers in solving “tricky mental tests” like quests and killing rats in a recent Canadian study of 100 University of Toronto students. Heh, it doesn’t really say that, but the article is a bit vague on the type of quests puzzles mental tasks involved. In other news, gamers are also better at jumping on ledges and scamming.
