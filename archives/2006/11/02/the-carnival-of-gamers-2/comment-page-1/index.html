The Carnival of Gamers

Posted on November 2, 2006 by Foton

You kids are gonna have to keep it down out here today. Uncle Foton’s feeling a little weak and until he takes a nap at the office, he doesn’t want to hear any roughhousing or loud arguments.

Fortunately, today is the November Carnival of Gamers with lots of stuff to keep you entertained.

The Carnival of Gamers is a nomadic monthly showcase of articles by gamers, for gamers and about gamers. Next month’s host will be Surly Gamers.

When you’ve finished consuming the Carnival, go read the U.S. Constitution. There’ll be a quiz tomorrow.
