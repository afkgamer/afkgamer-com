Improved Magic Technology

Posted on January 20, 2005 by Foton

The Warcraft Menethil-Auberdine boat is being replaced by NPC Porters, because of some ridiculous reason that sounds made up to me, but what would I know — I’m a user, not a provider. Excerpt, quoting Blizz dude, Tyren:

The reason for the maintenance is that we have identified that one of the problems causing multiple disconnects revolves around the boat system in the game. Specifically, the boat that travels between Menethil Harbor and Auberdine was causing errors that impacted stability for the entire realm.

When the realms come back online, this boat will be replaced by NPCs who can teleport players between these two locations. Once this fix is implemented, we will analyze performance under live conditions and from that point roll out the fix across all realms.

The boat may be restored some day to the game, maybe when the summer interns arrive, who knows. Of course, we all know what this means …

(My MS Paint skills are … lacking. Maybe when AFK Gamer’s imaginary summer intern arrives, I’ll have him spend a day or two improving that.)

Random Convo

Posted on January 20, 2005 by Foton

I was talking to this guy at work that plays Warcraft also. (Ya, scary.) We were chuckling about the forums and how some of the huge issues that come along, nary a blue (Blizz employee) response. But some asslicker posts how much he likes the game and THANK YOU LORD JESUS for Blizzard and their fabulous game, there’s a blue response all over that like stink on shit.

It’s only human nature, after all, to need and crave accolades at times, but this isn’t kindergarten. I assume people are being paid money to monitor the forums, and I further assume that these hires are adults, so how about focusing on the issues that matter instead of the stage bows?

We’ve got a /bug function that’s inoperative, a Chinese farmer infestation like the scabies (if you believe the forum posters’ anecdotal figures), an Auction House that apparently can’t do basic math AND is stealing money and items, characters sailing through the 50s with perma-rested experience, et fucking al — if you’re looking for hard responses to any of that, you’ve got a lot of hope.

Admittedly, keeping up with the forums when there’s 600,000 voices crying out is damn near impossible — but if I, fool that I am, can successfully skim over the tired and trite “Top Signs that Ur Playing 2 Much Warcraft!1!11” and find the potential gamebreakers, I think they can too.

Cripes, even a “I’ll ask about this” response would suffice. Well, and then actually ask about it and followup. The added bonus is that the post is then tagged with Blue so that dopes like me can follow the issue to its ultimate resolution.

Ya, ya. I’ve got a lot of hope.
