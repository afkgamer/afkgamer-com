The Burning Crusade Release Date

Posted on November 9, 2006 by Foton
5

NOVEMBER 17, 2006

No, wait. That’s the James Bond release date. Let’s do this again.

JANUARY 16, 2007

Here’s the press release so you can see I’m not just making that up. They call it a “street date” tho, which does sound way cooler.

Anyways, that’s 9.5 weeks to slog through raids of old content. …. 9 … and a half …. weeks ….

Also, you might want to have a look at how Blizzard is tuning the experience curve in the Burning Crusade beta per our buddy, Tigole:

–Lengthening the curve a bit
–Placing more emphasis on Quest Reward Experience
–Placing more emphasis on Dungeon Experience
–Placing less emphasis on grinding outdoor creatures while not on a quest

Tell me that’s not EQ.

Posers

Posted on November 9, 2006 by Foton
10

Here’s a Warcraft forum thread I really wish I hadn’t looked at: “Ever Seen Another Character W/ Your Name?” With the cross-server battlegrounds, I had known this was a possibility and had wondered how freaky that would be, to round a corner and run ker-smack into your opposite-faction self.

Depending on how freaked the other guy was, I’d probably waste precious stealthing time thinking “what in the hell is this????” and griping about posers in Ventrilo.

THEN, I read Reply #10 which suggested people do a character search on WarcraftRealms for their names. SONUVA … there’s 12 other level 60s with my main’s character name. A paladin! And, an even larger shame, a night elf hunter!

Ok that was bad, but this is worse: Raster did a search on his name and found a level 60 horde on our old server (our guild transferred away from that server to flee the lag) that was guilded with our former arch PVP rivals. (There was much cursing.)

Fucking posers. We carefully craft our game names and build multi-year, multi-game reputations, for better or worse, and they skate in and probably do all manner of stupid shit: stand in Rain of Fire, go toe to toe with a cleaving mob, taking a Locust Swarm in the face … ARE THEY EVEN VANISHING ON VAEL??

And one last thing, who’s the joker that made a character Fotonrogue on Kul Tiras???

To Ding or Not to Ding

Posted on November 9, 2006 by Foton
4

Feran level 13 paladin of the Warcraft realm, Scilla, doesn’t care if you ding, he just wants you to do it quietly. Quoting from the forum thread, “People who say ‘Ding'”: “… I understand that saying “ding” has been passed down from previous MMORPG’s. But really, do people crave that much attention that they have to let the whole world know every time they level. I’m sorry but the fact that you leveled is nothing special, it’s happening to everyone. …” Ya, ya, low levels shouting “Ding!” in a public channel is stupid, but I’m more inclined to agree with Reply #11 by Ravenhurst (level 60 hunter of Shattered Hand): “Ding is a goddamn tradition. If you don’t like it then get the hell out of my genre before you get trained by Fansy.” Tradition! Don’t mess with it, noob! And with the level cap rising to 70 in a few months, there’s gonna be a whole lot of dinging going on. (Archive of WoW Forum – People who say Ding, page 1)
