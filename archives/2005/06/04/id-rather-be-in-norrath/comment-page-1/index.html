I’d Rather be in Norrath

Posted on June 4, 2005 by Foton

I just can’t get enough of sign fakery. (make your own tombstone)

I have a couple of e-friends with real gaming-themed license plates. Ya, twisted and weird … and, it’s a bitch when max level is raised and they have to change the plates to reflect their uberness.

One of the e-thugs tried to get “FUEQ” approved from his DMV, but was turned down — probably the DMV thought it was a clever misspelling of fuck, but you and I know better, don’t we?

(make your own fake plate)

I think I’ve almost won Guild Wars. I dunno, I’m guessing here.

Big problem though: one of the guild dudes made the call this week … he’s had it, he’s formulating his exit plan and will be returning to some other virtual world, although which one he hasn’t decided yet.

He has been tossing around the “E” word alot. You know the one I mean. EverQuest.

I’ve been down this road before. It always starts out like this. A joke, a taunt, a throwaway idea. Next thing I know, I’m back on duty in Norrath.

And I’ll need a new license plate.
