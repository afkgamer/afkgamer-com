Improved Magic Technology

Posted on January 20, 2005 by Foton

The Warcraft Menethil-Auberdine boat is being replaced by NPC Porters, because of some ridiculous reason that sounds made up to me, but what would I know — I’m a user, not a provider. Excerpt, quoting Blizz dude, Tyren:

The reason for the maintenance is that we have identified that one of the problems causing multiple disconnects revolves around the boat system in the game. Specifically, the boat that travels between Menethil Harbor and Auberdine was causing errors that impacted stability for the entire realm.

When the realms come back online, this boat will be replaced by NPCs who can teleport players between these two locations. Once this fix is implemented, we will analyze performance under live conditions and from that point roll out the fix across all realms.

The boat may be restored some day to the game, maybe when the summer interns arrive, who knows. Of course, we all know what this means …
