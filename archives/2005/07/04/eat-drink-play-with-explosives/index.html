Eat, Drink, Play with Explosives

Posted on July 4, 2005 by Foton

The fireworks ordinances in World of Warcraft’s Ironforge are very liberal. How many times does Ironforge have to burn to the ground before we get some safety measures in place?!?

And, would it kill them to put up some guard railings along the Molten Span chain bridges? Shit, just summon my ass to the zone — after a couple of cocktails, there is NO WAY I can negotiate my way safely over a narrow, slimey bridge.

Anyways. In a fitting tribute to America and her historical ties to capitalism, I noticed that some assrag had put some stacks of fireworks for sale in the Crack House … stack of 5, only cost ya 1 gold and saves the walk across the bridge to the NPC vendor.

That’s American, too.
