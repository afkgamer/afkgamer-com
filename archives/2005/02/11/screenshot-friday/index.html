Screenshot Friday

Posted on February 11, 2005 by Foton

Spanning the World of Warcraft …

This is Hannah. She lives in Lakeshire in the Redridge Mountains. Not to be insensitive, but damn, that is one ugly kid.

Some noob kept asking in the public channel where to find a Lakeshire NPC. Even though he was told, in great detail, several times, where to find said NPC, searching around inside the Inn proved too much for him.

Some Westfall noob asked how to summon his warlock voidwalker, and one of my guildmates (Mate01) kindly responded that he needed a soul shard. A level 20 warlock that didn’t know where to get a soul shard. Ya. Scary.

Foton explains it all. My priest alt took on a few too many gnolls and hit the dirt right in front of a small group of onlookers. The shame of it!!

When creating the army of alts, one guildmate decided to try out the other gender and is having a little too much fun with that.

I was bypassing content in the Zul’FuckedUp instance. Didn’t do a very good job of it, though, as evidenced by the dozens of newly-spawned trolls in my way.

Remember kids, don’t bypass content like Foton does. And if you must, do a better job of it.
