Is it a Crime if it’s Just Pixels?

Posted on February 14, 2005 by Foton

Over at Broken Toys, Lum and his imaginary assistants found an “attempted” interview of IGE’s pharming mogul, Steve Salyer, and, inadvertently, of IGE mouthpiece, Eric Duncan.

Excerpt:

Question: Over the years both IGE and its business has grown, how many employees are currently working for IGE?

Answer – Steve (Salyer): We have about 150 employees/consultants at the moment.

#1 – Right now IGE’s tax accountants are shitting bricks for that “150 employees” comment. (I’m sure he really meant to say there’s one employee (him) and 149 consultants … which require no payroll taxes. Or benefits. And don’t even think about the company 401(k).)

#2 – Consultants sounds nicer than whores or farmers, doesn’t it?

#3 – I wonder if Salyer takes the best stuff for himself. If I was him, I would. “I’ll take 3 of those, 16 of those quest items, 5 of those and I’ll need one of the Onyxia keys. Thanks.”

#4 – I just know the gaming Poindexters are incredulous right now at the suggestion that uber guilds are reselling to IGE. (The very idea!) Welcome to late 1999.

Heh, as I think about it, I’ll bet those Onyxia keys aren’t tradeable, which amply demonstrates how far away I am from worrying about Onyxia AND that I’d make a piss-poor IGE “consultant.”
