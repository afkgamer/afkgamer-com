Warcraft Night Elves have an Attitude Problem

Posted on November 15, 2005 by Foton

What is it the with NPC night elves in Warcraft?

Almost without exception, assholes, every one of ’em. Since player interaction with these pricks is limited to clicking “accept” or “continue” or “gimme my reward”, there’s no possibility of toying with them like in other games … kinda sucks the fun out of it.
