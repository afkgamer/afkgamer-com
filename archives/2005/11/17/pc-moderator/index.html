PC Moderator

Posted on November 17, 2005 by Foton

With all the talk about computer gaming addictions this week (AS ADDICTIVE AS CRACK! CRAAACK!!!), concerned parents and other loved ones might want to invest in a PC Moderator which regulates computer use by killing the video signal after the set time has expired. Although, since it only requires a small phillips screwdriver to install, wouldn’t it only require a small phillips screwdriver to UNinstall? I think teens have cracked the technology behind the phillips screwdriver, but maybe I’m giving them more credit than they deserve.
